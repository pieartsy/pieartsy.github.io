Art

I'm a self-taught digital illustrator and comic creator, primarily focused on character art. My work has been published in several zines and I do commissions. Further examples of my art are on my various social media accounts @pieartsy; you can find links to those in the upper right corner of my website. More information on my commissions can be found here: Commissions.
